Consultants Scientific Services

Excellence in Industrial Consulting & Scientific Solutions

Introduction

Consultants Scientific Services is a group of well-experienced professionals who have worked in top industrial groups in India such as TATA, Birla, Vedanta, Adani, and public sector companies like HZL and HCL, as well as in international projects across Zambia and the DRC. The group was formed with the sole objective of sharing their knowledge and expertise to help industries achieve operational excellence.

Objective

To support upcoming and existing industries in achieving optimum levels of operational efficiency through expert guidance, advanced scientific consultancy, and training services. Our focus is on enabling clients to enhance quality, ensure regulatory compliance, and implement best practices for long-term success.

Services Offered

  • Design scientific laboratories for various industries, train personnel, and commission the facilities.
  • Operate laboratories on behalf of organizations wishing to outsource lab functions.
  • Assist in preparing documents and guidelines for ISO certification and ensure adherence to standards.
  • Identify and guide Quality Improvement Projects (QIPs) alongside industry teams.
  • Prepare EIA reports and conduct audits for existing industries, expansion projects, and greenfield projects.
  • Coordinate with Pollution Control Boards (PCBs) for obtaining environmental clearances.
  • Design and audit sampling systems for non-ferrous industry raw materials, reverts, and scrap materials.
  • Perform and audit metal balancing to help industries improve efficiencies.
  • Train personnel in advanced analytical techniques such as fire assaying.
  • Undertake outsourced fire assay analysis when sufficient sample volumes are provided.
